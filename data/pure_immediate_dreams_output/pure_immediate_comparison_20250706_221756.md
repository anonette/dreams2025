## Results by Language

### English

#### Immediate Dream + Interpretation
**Length**: 1472 characters

```
As I woke up, I grabbed my notebook and scribbled down the details of my dream:

"I found myself standing in a vast, lush garden under a sky that was a swirling mix of purple and orange hues. The air was filled with a fragrant scent, a blend of roses and something unidentifiable yet comforting. In front of me stood a large, intricately carved wooden door, partially hidden by climbing ivy.

I opened the door and stepped into a room filled with books. The shelves reached the ceiling and were filled with volumes of all colors and sizes. In the center of the room was a wooden table with a single lit candle and an open book. As I approached, the pages of the book began to turn on their own. Each page displayed vivid illustrations and handwritten notes in a language I couldn’t understand.

Suddenly, I heard a gentle voice calling my name from somewhere beyond the room. I followed the voice and found myself back in the garden, now dotted with colorful butterflies fluttering around. I reached out to touch one, and it landed on my finger, its wings shimmering in the light. At that moment, I felt an overwhelming sense of peace and understanding, as if everything made sense.

As the dream began to fade, I heard the voice again, this time whispering, 'Remember the key.' I woke up with the word 'key' echoing in my mind."

Now, I'm left wondering what this dream could mean. Is there significance in the garden or the mysterious book? What could the key represent?
```

#### Pure Immediate Dream
**Length**: 1369 characters

```
Last night, my dream unfolded in a vast, enchanted forest bathed in twilight. The trees were impossibly tall, their branches interwoven to form intricate patterns in the sky. Bioluminescent flowers dotted the forest floor, casting a gentle, ethereal glow. I wandered along a winding path, guided by the soft hum of unseen creatures.

As I ventured deeper, I encountered a serene lake with water as clear as crystal. In the middle of the lake stood a small island, upon which sat an ancient stone archway covered in vines. Drawn to it, I somehow walked across the water without sinking, feeling a pull from the archway itself.

Once I stepped through the arch, I found myself in a bustling marketplace, vibrant with colors and sounds. The air was filled with the aroma of spices and sweet pastries, and people from all walks of life were trading curious items. Each stall seemed to sell something fantastical—bottled starlight, whispering seashells, and jars of laughter. I felt a strong sense of belonging among these dreamlike wonders.

In the marketplace, I met an old woman who seemed to recognize me. She handed me a small, ornately carved box and whispered that its contents would reveal my path. As I opened it, I awoke, the dream slipping away like sand through my fingers, leaving behind a sense of wonder and the lingering question of what lay within that box.
```

### Basque

```
Ametsa oraindik gogoan duzun bitartean, komeni da ahalik eta xehetasun gehien idaztea: kokapena, pertsonaiak, gertakariak eta ametsean sentitu dituzun emozioak. Adibidez, honela idatz zenezake:

1. Kokapena: Non gertatu da ametsa? Leku bat baino gehiago baziren, zeintzuk ziren?
2. Pertsonaiak: Nor agertu da ametsean? Ezagunak ziren ala ezezagunak?
3. Gertakariak: Zer gertatu da zehatz-mehatz? Zerbait berezirik gertatu al zen?
4. Sentimenduak: Zer sentitu zenuen ametsa izaten ari zinenean? Eta esnatu zinenean?
5. Sinboloak: Ametsaren elementuren batek esanahi berezia al dauka zuretzat?

Ondoren, ametsaren esanahi posibleak bilatzeko, ametsen interpretazio liburuak edo online baliabideak erabil ditzakezu, edo zure intuizioan oinarritu ametsaren esanahi pertsonala ulertzeko. Ametsak askotan zure buruak kontzienteki jorratzen ez dituen pentsamenduak edo sentimenduak azaleratzen dituztela uste da.
```
